Art has always represented a major part of my life and it shaped me into the person that I am today, an individual with high values, moral standards, and openness to any new concept or theory. From my point of view, it can be easily regarded as the highest form of expression, which allows one to convey his or her feelings in their own unique way.

A bit about myself

I am currently in my first year of university, at FSPAC (Faculty of Political, Administrative and Communication Sciences) studying Digital Media in Cluj-Napoca. Therefore, I am specializing in Web Design, Graphic Design, Media production but also Journalism.

I am accustomed to the Adobe Creative Suite, including Photoshop, Illustrator and Premiere Pro, having worked with them for the last 2 years for both personal and professional projects. I am also skilled in social media marketing and PR as I had the opportunity to work with Instagram and Facebook tools.

WHY THIS PATH?

I have always been ardent about creating art, more specifically, pieces of work that show my true character and are filled with mystery and emotion. The most intriguing part of this vast artistic domain, in my vision, is undoubtedly represented by graphic design, which manages to illustrate and capture various aspects of one's everyday life giving them a deeper meaning.
